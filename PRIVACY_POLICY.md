# Privacy Policy for SoberQuest

**Last Updated: January 20, 2026**

## Introduction

SoberQuest ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application SoberQuest (the "App"). Please read this privacy policy carefully. If you do not agree with the terms of this privacy policy, please do not access the App.

## Information We Collect

### Information You Provide to Us

**Addiction Tracking Data**
- Types of addictions you choose to track
- Sobriety start dates
- Progress reset dates (if you choose to reset your progress)
- Daily check-in records
- Personal motivation text
- Badge unlock achievements

**Usage Data**
- App interaction data
- Badge unlock events
- Feature usage patterns
- Session duration and frequency

### Information Collected Automatically

**Device Information**
- Device type and model
- Operating system version
- Unique device identifiers
- App version

**Analytics Data**
- App performance metrics
- Crash reports and diagnostics
- Feature usage statistics

### Information from Third Parties

**Subscription Management (via Superwall and Apple)**
- Subscription status
- Purchase history
- Trial usage status
- Subscription expiration dates

**Push Notifications (via Apple Push Notification Service)**
- Device push tokens (if notifications are enabled)
- Notification preferences

## How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

1. **Provide Core Functionality**
   - Track your sobriety progress
   - Calculate milestone achievements
   - Unlock and display badges
   - Maintain your check-in streaks
   - Display your personal motivation

2. **Improve User Experience**
   - Analyze app usage patterns
   - Fix bugs and technical issues
   - Develop new features
   - Optimize app performance

3. **Manage Subscriptions**
   - Process subscription purchases
   - Manage trial periods
   - Verify subscription status
   - Handle subscription renewals and cancellations

4. **Send Notifications** (with your permission)
   - Trial reminder notifications
   - Badge unlock notifications
   - Motivational messages
   - Check-in reminders

5. **Comply with Legal Obligations**
   - Respond to legal requests
   - Enforce our terms and policies
   - Protect user safety and security

## Data Storage and Security

### Local Data Storage

**On-Device Storage**
- All your sobriety tracking data (addictions, progress, badges, check-ins) is stored locally on your device using secure iOS UserDefaults
- Your data remains private and is not automatically shared with our servers
- Uninstalling the app will delete all locally stored data

### Data Security Measures

We implement appropriate technical and organizational security measures to protect your information, including:
- Encryption of data in transit using industry-standard SSL/TLS protocols
- Secure data storage using iOS security features
- Regular security assessments
- Access controls and authentication

However, please note that no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your personal information, we cannot guarantee its absolute security.
